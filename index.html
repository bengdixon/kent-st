Context

Kent Street sits between systems that do not naturally align.

  • Clinical decision-making
  • Commissioning and funding
  • Care delivery
  • Regulatory oversight
  • Patient and family trust
Photography
02
The Gap

Patients are ready to leave hospital.

But discharge is delayed because:

  • No suitable step-down environment
  • Risk concerns from clinicians
  • Unclear responsibility after discharge
  • Limited specialist placements
03
The Decision Moment

A clinician has a patient ready for discharge.

They must decide: is there a safe, appropriate place for this person to go?

What they need to know immediately

  • Is this patient suitable for Kent Street?
  • Who takes responsibility after discharge?
  • Is the environment safe and structured?
  • How quickly can placement happen?

What happens if this is not clear

  • Discharge is delayed
  • Hospital stay continues
  • Cost increases
  • Patient progress stalls
04
The Result

When discharge fails, the cost compounds.

+
Extended hospital stays
+
Increased system cost
+
Delayed patient progress

Pressure builds across services.

05
The Solution

Kent Street provides a structured alternative.

  • Safe, staffed environments
  • Clear handover and responsibility
  • Defined care pathways
  • Placements designed around individual need
